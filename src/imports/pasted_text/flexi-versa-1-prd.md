Flexi Versa 1 - Product Requirements Document
(PRD)
1. Product Overview
Flexi Versa 1 is a unified customer acquisition and engagement platform that integrates marketing,
communication, CRM, voice, and analytics into a single ecosystem.
2. Problem Statement
Businesses currently use disconnected tools for marketing, communication, CRM, and analytics,
leading to missed leads, slow response times, and poor visibility into conversions.
3. Product Vision
To create a centralized platform that enables businesses to attract, capture, engage, and convert users
efficiently across multiple channels.
4. Target Users
Marketing teams, Sales teams, Customer support teams, Business owners, CRM administrators.
5. Core Modules
1. Marketing Hub – campaign and traffic management 2. Lead Capture Hub – forms, WhatsApp,
chatbot 3. Communication Hub – WhatsApp, Email, SMS 4. CRM Integration Hub – API-based syncing
5. Voice Hub – IVR and automated calls 6. Analytics Hub – reporting and dashboards
6. Functional Requirements
- Capture leads from forms, WhatsApp, QR, campaigns - Automate messaging workflows - Sync data
with CRM systems - Track campaign and conversion metrics - Provide real-time dashboard visibility
7. User Flows
Flow 1: Ad ® WhatsApp ® Lead ® CRM ® Follow-up ® Conversion Flow 2: Website Form ® Lead
® CRM ® Reminder ® Conversion Flow 3: QR Code ® Landing Page ® Lead ® Automation ®
Analytics
8. CRM Integration
Supports Zoho CRM, Salesforce CRM, HubSpot CRM, and custom systems. Integration via APIs and
webhooks with lead mapping and assignment.
9. Analytics & Reporting
- Conversion rates - Campaign performance - Response times - Lead source tracking - ROI metrics
10. Limitations
- WhatsApp requires template approvals and opt-in - CRM APIs may have rate limits - Voice solutions
depend on infrastructure - Compliance varies by region
11. Success Metrics
- Lead conversion rate - Response time - Cost per lead - Campaign ROI - CRM sync accuracy
12. MVP Scope
- Dashboard - Lead management - Campaign page - WhatsApp automation - CRM integration view -
Analytics page
